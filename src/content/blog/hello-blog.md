I have wanted a place to write about what I am building for a while now. The external sites — Narvik, Extraction, The Red Soil — are for long-form creative and political writing. This space is for something different: shorter thoughts on technology, open-source, and the process of making things.

## What this is for

Expect posts about:

- Tooling and workflows I find useful
- Things I learned while building [jot](https://github.com/mooship/jot) or [Elekti](https://elekti.timothybrits.co.za/)
- Opinions on software that are too long for a status update

## What this is not

It is not a newsletter, a tutorial site, or a portfolio. It is a notebook with the lid left open.
