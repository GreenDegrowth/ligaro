Artemis II has me deeply excited about space again. Humans are going back to the Moon -- actually going, with a crew on board -- and it's the first time that's happened since Apollo. Something about it just hits different when there are people in the capsule.

It also happens to coincide with a new season of For All Mankind, which feels almost too perfect. Between the real mission and the show, I've been thinking about space constantly. The timing couldn't be better.

To the Moon.
